Clara: Your Image Genie

Search your local pictures with a keyword just like a Google Image Search!

Clara is a local picture search and insight assistant with data privacy

Clara lets you search through your photo album with a single keyword, just like a Google Image Search!

It can be quite difficult to search through your entire camera roll to find a single image but Clara lets you input a search term and will return either:

- an image description (alt-text) of all pictures with a match
- all pictures containing the specified object
- all pictures containing the specific word provided
